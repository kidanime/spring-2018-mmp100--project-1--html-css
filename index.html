Satoru Iwata born on December 6, 1959 in, Sapporo, Japan. Satoru was taking interested in computers and video games when he was in middele school with a demo computer that used telephone lines. During his junior year of high school Satoru developed his own games on his HP-56 the first programmable claculator several games he produced were simple number games such as, volleyball and Missile Attack. His first computer that he had was a Commodore PET which he dismantaled it to study how to works. Coincidentally the computer had the same central processing unit that the Nintendo Entertainment System (NES) had that he would later devlop games for. After high school he was admitted to Tokyo institute of Technology where he majored in computer science.

Satoru Iwata by cbc.ca

While attending university in 1980 Satoru Iwata worked for HAL Laboratory as a part-time programmer in two years after he graduated he joined the company full-time and he became the fifth employee and their only programmer. In 1983 Satoru became the comapny's coordinator of software production and during that time he helped HAL develop a relationship with Nintendo so they would be able to develop games for NIntendoes newly released NES. He requested permision to work on games for NES to which Nintendo agreed to. He worked on Balloon Fight, NES Open Tournament Golf, EarthBound, and the Kirby games,etc. Even thouth Satoru wasn't part of Nintendo at the time he did assist in the development of Pokemon Gold and Silver. According to the president of The Pokemon Company Satoru Iwata was a instrument in bringing Pokemon to the Western markets. In addition to all this he also helped in the development of Super Smash Bros.

In 2000 Satoru Iwata took a position at Nintendo as the head of its corporate planning division and took a seat on the board of directors two years later wehn Yamauchi, the company's president since 1949, retired from Nintendo on MAY 24, 2002. Iwata became the fourth president of the Nintendo company with yamauchi final request "that Nintendo give birth to wholly new ideas and create hardware which reflects that ideal. And make software that adheres to that same standard." at the time of his promotion Nintendo wasn't performing as wel as the other console makers, with GameCube doing poorly and Sony's playstation 2 and Microsoft'S XBOX outselling the others Satoru took cautions when approching the issue.Few years passed and Iwata invented the Nintendo DS,DS Lite, DSI,the WII,WII U,and the Nintendo Switch. By July 11, 2015 Satoru Iwata died due to compications from a tumor at age 55.
